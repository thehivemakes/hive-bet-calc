Understanding Bookmaker Hold (Vig)

The hold (also called vig, juice, or overround) is the bookmaker's built-in profit margin. It is the amount by which implied probabilities exceed 100%.

A standard -110/-110 line implies each side has a 52.4% chance of winning, totaling 104.8%. The 4.8% excess is the hold. Lower hold means better value for bettors.
