How Parlay Bets Work

A parlay combines multiple bets into one. All legs must win for the parlay to pay out. The odds multiply together, creating higher potential payouts but lower probability of winning.

To calculate: convert each leg to decimal odds, multiply them all together, then multiply by your stake. The implied probability of all legs winning equals the product of each leg's individual probability.
