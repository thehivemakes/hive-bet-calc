What Is Arbitrage Betting?

Arbitrage betting exploits differences in odds between bookmakers to guarantee a profit regardless of the outcome. It works when the implied probabilities across all outcomes sum to less than 100%.

To check for arbitrage: convert all odds to decimal, sum 1/odds for each outcome. If the total is less than 1, arbitrage exists and the difference is your profit margin.
