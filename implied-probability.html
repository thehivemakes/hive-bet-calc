Understanding Implied Probability

Implied probability is the probability of an outcome as reflected by the betting odds. It tells you what the market believes about the likelihood of each outcome.

To find value bets, compare implied probability to your own estimated probability. If you believe a team has a 60% chance of winning but the odds imply only 50%, that may be a value bet.
